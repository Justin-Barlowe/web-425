Who was Jeff Lindsay?

Jeff Lindsay is an American author, best known for creating the character Dexter Morgan, a serial killer who targets other murderers. Dexter became the basis of the hit TV series "Dexter." Born in 1952, Lindsay has a background in drama, having been a playwright and actor. His work often explores dark and intricate psychological themes. Lindsay's gripping narrative style and unique character development have made him a standout figure in contemporary crime and thriller literature.
